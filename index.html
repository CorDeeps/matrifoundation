About Us

Matrifoundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting underprivileged students through scholarships and educational assistance. We believe that every child deserves a chance at quality education, regardless of their financial background.

Our Scholarship Program

We offer scholarships to students from economically weaker sections who demonstrate academic potential and determination. Through partnerships with schools and donors, we ensure that deserving students get the support they need to continue their education.

Join us in making a difference. Whether you're a student in need, a donor, or a volunteer, your support helps us reach more lives.
