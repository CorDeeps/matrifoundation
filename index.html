About Us

Mohit Joshi NGO is committed to transforming lives through education and healthcare initiatives in underserved communities.

Education

Quality Education for All

We focus on providing quality education to underprivileged children, offering scholarships, school supplies, and digital learning tools. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by empowering young minds with knowledge and skills.

  • Scholarship programs
  • Community libraries
  • Teacher training workshops
  • Digital classrooms

Healthcare

Accessible Healthcare for Everyone

Our healthcare programs aim to improve access to essential medical services in rural areas. We organize health camps, vaccination drives, and awareness sessions to promote preventive care and healthy living.

  • Mobile health clinics
  • Vaccination drives
  • Health awareness workshops
  • Maternal and child health support
